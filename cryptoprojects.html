Crypto Projects

A current project that I am involved in is De-Fi Logic as an Analyst. This is a "Think Tank" organization, in which we study existing and emerging crypto projects and share our findings with our members. Many of these members are people who are not technologically savy, and can initially struggle with understanding some of these concepts, let alone understanding De-centralized finance. This experience has honed my skill set in explaining technical subjects to non-technically minded individuals.

In December of 2013 I saw a project, that I came to believe in. The community was so vibrant and wanting to change the world. This project is of course Dogecoin. Watching this enthusiastic and good-will fueled community it was impossible not to join. Aside from mining in the early days when it was still possible with a simple desktop computer, I also created one of the earliset unoffical "Dogecoin" Facebook groups, attempting to spread awareness about the project.
